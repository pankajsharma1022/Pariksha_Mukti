Welcome to ParikshaMukti, a comprehensive platform designed to facilitate academic collaboration and resource sharing among students from various universities.

Our website provides access to a vast collection of previous years' exam papers from numerous institutions, allowing students to prepare effectively for their upcoming exams.

Beyond just an archive, our platform encourages student interaction and collaboration through project sharing features, enabling users to work together on academic and extracurricular projects.

Additionally, our marketplace feature offers a convenient space for buying and selling notes, further supporting the academic journey. Embracing the open-source ethos, we invite contributions from students and educators alike to continuously expand and improve our repository, ensuring it remains a valuable and evolving educational resource for all.
