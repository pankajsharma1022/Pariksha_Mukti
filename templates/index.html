About Us

Welcome to ParikshaMukti, your one-stop destination for accessing previous years' exam papers from universities around the globe. We are an open-source platform dedicated to making academic resources easily accessible to students everywhere.

Our Mission

At ParikshaMukti, we believe in democratizing education by providing students with free and easy access to essential academic resources. Our mission is to create a collaborative space where students from all backgrounds can find exam papers, work together on projects, and exchange notes, helping each other succeed.
